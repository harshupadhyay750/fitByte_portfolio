Flagship Project

fitByte — AI Fitness & Nutrition

A smart, intuitive health companion that tracks calories, macros, and water intake while using AI to plan vegetarian diet strategies.

Intuitive Login & Onboarding

Elegant design welcome interface built for effortless onboarding, keeping users engaged from the very first tap.

Multi-Account Google Sign-In

Seamlessly integrated Firebase OAuth supporting multiple accounts with secure, quick sign-in profiles.

Smart Dashboard Tracker

Provides a clean calorie breakdown: Goals, Eaten (e.g. dal chawal), Burned, and macro rings tracking Protein, Carbs, and Fats in real time.

AI Diet Planner

Generates targeted calorie strategies (like Weight Loss Deficit) and maps out required macronutrients (179g Protein, 239g Carbs, 79g Fat).

Real-time Dashboard Sync

Automated cloud sync dynamically monitors logs and prompts helpful sync feedback banners e.g., "You have reached 17% of your daily goal."
